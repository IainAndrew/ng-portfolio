Web Design

This project started life as a rough mockup in Illustrator. The client envisioned large, full width blocks of colour and images, with striking text and big, eyecatching CTA buttons.

The fonts I decided on for this website are Baufra for headlines and buttons, and Cabrito for the body copy. I felt that this was a nice pairing which suited the aesthetics of the site well.

For the 'Showcase' section, the goal was to display all the projects as a grid, with each one expanding to full screen when clicked. On hover, each item has an overlay displaying the name of the project, and what role Plugmedia played in its creation.

Each 'Work' project item, when expanded, opens a image slideshow, with an 'info' button at the bottom of the screen to display a short description of the project.

I decided to place a 'contact us' block with a call to action at the end of every page, to give the user an opportunity to get in contact once they've finished reading the content.
