Logo Design

The client was looking for a logo that was not only clean and modern, but also represented the brand well, was recognisable and scaled down well. After trying out a few different concepts, I came up with the circular plug icon.

I designed the icon in Illustrator, using the pen tool.

The font I chose for the main text of the logo is Coolvetica Regular, and the tagline is Baufra Regular. I wanted a nice thick, eyecatching font which paired well with the icon.

On the website, I ended up implementing the icon and the logo in SVG and pure css, respectively.
