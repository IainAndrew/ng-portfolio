Web Design

Peter was looking for a simple, clean, and modern website to showcase his talents. We agreed that a single page scrolling website would be perfect for his needs. I started by creating a rough mockup in Illustrator.

For the navigation, I decided on a minimal, icon based navigation at the top left, which collapses into a dropdown menu for smaller devices.

I used fixed background images for the studio photos on the page for a subtle effect, and also to save some vertical space.

The fonts used are MTT Milano Bold for headings and buttons, and MTT Milano Regular for body copy.

The page resolves nicely with the 'contact' section, featuring the same blue background as the header, and an elegant, minimal contact form.

We decided that a grid style Instagram feed at the bottom would round out the design nicely, to give prospective clients an insight into Peter's personality as well as his work.
