Trunkys

Interface design & fun illustrations for a mobile app.

Trunkys is a mobile app concept which could allow regular people to utilise the extra space in their car to deliver packages on their commute.

I was requested to create a series of colourful illustrations to show the app in use, as well as design a logo and user interface for the app itself.

Built With:

  • Adobe Illustrator
