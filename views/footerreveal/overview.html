Footer-reveal.js

A jQuery plugin for quick implementation of a revealing footer effect.

The footer reveal effect is one I come across quite often in modern web design. I love this effect, and so I decided to write a jQuery plugin for people to quickly add this functionality to their footer element.

I felt that it could be useful for beginners, or for people who simply want a fast way of implementing the effect on a fluid-height footer (not really achievable in pure CSS).
