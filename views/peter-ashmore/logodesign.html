Logo Design

Peter requested a strong, striking text-based logo to suit the aesthetics of the rest of the site.

After going back and forth with a few different ideas, I arrived on the logo below.

The fonts used are ARS Maquette Pro Black (with a subtle text shadow) and MTT Milano Bold. We both felt that this logo suited the site well and made for a great looking header.

As the logo was entirely text based, I implemented it on the site with pure CSS.
